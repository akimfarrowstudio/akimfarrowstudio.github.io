Strange Harvest

YEAR 2024
MEDIUM WELDED STEEL, GLASS
SCALE 305 X 90 X 90 CM

This piece depicts harvest as structure: gathered, counted, and displayed. A tapered steel lattice rises from a circular base, its geometry precise and exposed. Cobalt bottles extend from branching rods at the crown, positioned like suspended yield.

The framework remains open and skeletal, with every weld visible. The bottles sit outward from the core, suggesting display, storage, and circulation rather than containment. The work examines how systems organize what is gathered and how value becomes architectural.
